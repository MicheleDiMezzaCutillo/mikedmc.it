Slogan

Looking for a unique way to relax? Forget the hustle and bustle of everyday life and let me do nothing to help you.

Why would you need me?

Have you ever struggled to find a friend to join you in doing nothing in particular? Or to play with others and feel like you have to “entertain” them by talking or doing something? Here I am, also known as the “Man Who Does Nothing.”

What Do I Do?

I offer a rather unconventional rental service to my online clients by committing to “Do Nothing.”

Why choose me?

Although there are agencies where you can hire actors to play your friend, or the whole family, such rental services are not rare. However, I stand out for a “light” approach rather than taking on a different character.

What do I mean by “Do Nothing”?

“Doing nothing means eating and drinking (with clients), playing with them, hanging out on Discord, and answering their simple questions with simple answers.”
